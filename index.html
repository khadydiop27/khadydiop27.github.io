About Us

Who We Are

As an all black female cohort from the University of Virginia, we are aware of disparities in representation in all fields of study. Specifically, the From English team wanted to address the disparity in the fields of language learning and computer science.

We decided to make the From English website as it is designed to tackle the unique challenges of learning a new language as a native English speaker. Our team is made up of the study of four different languages. Those languages are: Korean, Russian, Spanish, and Swahili. The From English team hopes to one day expand to even more languages around the world with the end goal of making language learning accessible for all.
